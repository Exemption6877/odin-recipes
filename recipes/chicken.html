Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe

Home

Description

This tangy, creamy Buffalo chicken dip tastes just like Buffalo chicken wings! Perfect for parties and game day, this chicken dip is best served hot with crackers and celery sticks. Everyone loves the results!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cream Cheese
  • Ranch Dressing
  • Hot Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the chicken and hot sauce in a skillet until heated through.
  2. Mix in the cream cheese and dressing.
  3. When the mixture is well-blended, add half of the cheese and stir until incorporated.
  4. Transfer the dip to a slow cooker.
  5. Sprinkle the remaining cheese on top and cook on Low until bubbly.
